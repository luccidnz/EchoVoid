How to Play

  • Move with Arrow Keys / WASD.
  • Collect coins and bonus drops, avoid enemies and hazards.
  • Press Space to trigger Overcharge when Charge is 100%.
  • Stopwatch slows enemies, Glove enables pulse punch, Bone grants armor, Volt-Carrot boosts speed + charge.
  • Portals warp you. Slime slows you. Trap tiles pulse lethal. Carrot missiles fly in lanes.
